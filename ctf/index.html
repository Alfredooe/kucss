KU Cyber Sec CTF Event

Kingston University Cyber Security Society is hosting a CTF event!

This will be a jeopardy-style competition with multiple challenge categories.

Taking place: 10am Thursday 28th - 23.59 Friday 29th April

New to CTFs?

No problem. This CTF is designed for all experience levels. Here is some information to help you understand how it works:

  • CTFs are information security competitions where you have to solve various tasks to obtain a flag.
  • Your goal is to submit as many flags as you can to earn points.
  • There will be a scoreboard of participants competing for the prizes.
