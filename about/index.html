About

Kingston University Cyber Security Society is student run, founded in 2020.

Our goal is to provide the students further learning and a chance to socialise in a friendly environment.

We will be hosting technical talks on various topics including: info sec, hacking, ethics, careers and more.

Weekly meetups every Wednesday @ 6.00pm

What do we do

  • Public speakers - attend lectures from people in industry
  • CTF competitions - show off your l33t hacking skills
  • Workshops - to teach and practice new cybersecurity skills
  • Study groups - to help people both on and off of the Cyber Security courses
  • Public events - we will be organising trips to public events in London
