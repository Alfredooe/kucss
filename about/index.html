About

We’re SIGINT, the cyber security society at The University of Edinburgh.

We host a variety of events from workshops and talks to Capture the Flag competitions. We compete across the country and place in the top 3 teams in the country.

Achievements

  • 1st Place InterACE 2018
  • 1st and 2nd Place CSC Scotland 2018
  • 1st Place DeloitteCTF 2018
  • 1st Place LTDH 2019
  • 1st Place Hackback2
  • 1st and 3rd Place DeloitteCTF 2019 Quals
  • 1st Place at DeloitteCTF 2019 Finals
  • 1st Place in HackTheBox Uni CTF Quals
  • 1st Place in HackTheBox Uni CTF 2021 Finals
  • 1st Place Le Tour du Hack 2021
